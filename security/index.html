We’re committed to ensuring the security of our users and their data. In this section, we outline the measures we take and what we do to protect your data.

To provide you with the best experience, we maintain a secure environment while also keeping our performance at the highest level. This includes ensuring that our servers are up and running smoothly, our software is up to date, and our security protocols are airtight. In short, we do everything we can to keep our platform secure and running smoothly.

Data Center Security

KayleAI is hosted in a Fasthosts Data Centre which is ISO 27001 certified. This means that the data centre is audited and certified to the highest standards of security and privacy. Access is strictly limited to cleared personnel with a role-based swipe-card system, and a sophisticated CCTV network covers all areas of our data centres and offices.
